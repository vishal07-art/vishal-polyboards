How a board is born

Waste in, clean board out.

Every reel follows the same four steps — so the board you reorder next month behaves exactly like the one you ordered today.

STEP 01

Collect & sort

Cardboard and paper waste is sourced, graded and sorted to remove contaminants before pulping.

STEP 02

Pulp & clean

Sorted stock is re-pulped, screened and cleaned into a uniform fibre slurry ready for forming.

STEP 03

Form & press

The slurry is layered, pressed and dried into board at the GSM and calliper your order specifies.

STEP 04

Finish & cut

Board is calendered, quality-checked, then cut to sheet size or wound to reels and dispatched.
