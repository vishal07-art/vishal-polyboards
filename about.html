Who we are

Built for the Sivakasi belt.
Rooted in Uppathur Vilakku.

Vishal Polyboards is a recycled paper and board mill that turns collected cardboard and paper waste into clean, consistent boards — supplied to calendar, notebook, diary and packaging manufacturers who call Sivakasi home.

Our story

From local waste to print-ready board

We started with a simple idea: the printing and packaging cluster around Sivakasi deserves a reliable local source of recycled board — made to the GSM, calliper and finish their lines actually need.

Today our mill at Uppathur Vilakku, Virudhunagar (PIN 626205), re-pulps sorted waste fibre into grey board, duplex, calendar board, diary covers and kraft — cut to sheet or wound to reel, and dispatched with the short transit times our customers depend on.

Mission

Consistent board, zero compromise on quality

To recycle cardboard and paper waste into print-ready boards that meet tight GSM and calliper specs — so calendar, notebook and packaging manufacturers can plan production with confidence, batch after batch.

Vision

South India's trusted recycled board partner

To become the go-to mill for the Sivakasi printing belt and beyond — known for reliable supply, honest pricing and a cleaner footprint built on 100% reclaimed fibre.

What drives us

Three things we never cut corners on

100% recycled fibre

Every grade we make comes from reclaimed material — less waste in landfills, a cleaner supply chain for you.

Reel-by-reel QC

Calliper and grammage checked on every reel so the board you reorder behaves exactly like the last batch.

Local to Sivakasi

Our customers are in the Sivakasi printing belt. Our mill is at Uppathur Vilakku — short transit, easy repeat orders.
